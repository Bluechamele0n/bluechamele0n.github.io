Angels

The Angels are the first creatures created by the gods of creation. They are 23 angels in total who was created by the gods where Fionil, Trill, Onigod and Flujack has five angels each and Tellemori has only created three.

In the hierarki of Celestial The gods of creation comes first due to them creating everything, then comes each gods highest angel, then there are Archangels who has celestial under them then under-angels and lastly Dragons who are seen as celestial.

The highest angel of each god is;
Destiny angel of Fionil.
Lucina angel of Trill.
Poki angel of Onigod.
Day and Night angels of Flujack.
Plantíos angel of Tellemori.

The Angels of Fionil, the goddess of time, space, mind and order in order of creation.
  • Clock, Angel of Time was the first angel to be created and he has the responsibility to stabilize the flow of time in The Time stream.
  • Known, Angel of Knowledge was the second angel of Fionil. He was made because she wanted everything to be recorded and there for Known, Angel of Knowledge has to write all into his endless library of knowledge.
  • Willia, Angel of Will was created so all could work by itself instead of all be controlled by the will of Fionil.
  • Filla, the Angel of Feelings was created without reason at first but when the creatures of the world came to be she was given the job to assist Willia, Angel of Will making all creatures work on their own.
  • Destiny, Archangel of Fate was the last angel to be created. He was made to control the paths all take and sew the fates of all so they reach the things the gods want them to do.
The Angels of Trill, The god of life, death, Divine and Dragons in order of creation.
  • Lucina, Archangel of Damnation she was at the beginning the angel of light and life, but when asked by Trill if she wanted to rule the afterlife she gladly accepted. She became the Lady of hell, Angel of Damnation and The Demonlord. She was also appointed to take care of some angels and help them with their tasks; Death, Angel of Passing,Undean, Angel of the Undead and Destiny, Archangel of Fate. She was also the second to be created and in that sense "adopted" Destiny, Archangel of Fate as her little brother.
  • Livius, Angel of Life was created after Lucina, Archangel of Damnation took over the Damnation and afterlife. He was given the responsibility of creating life.
  • Death, Angel of Passing was chosen as the one responsible of bringing the dead to the afterlife.
  • Drana, Archangel of Dragons and War was created to be responsible for the Dragons and Dragoné, Island of dragons. And she is also responsible for war.
    • Ignarion, the Firefather, The first dragon to be created and therefor the first celestial who isn't angel or god.
  • Prai, the Angel of Worship was made to communicate with the intelligent creatures of Fiondell.
The Angels of Onigod, The godess of Arcane, monsters and magic in order of creation.
  • Arcna, Angel of the Arcane was made to control and chose who gets magic.
  • Undean, Angel of the Undead was made to make sure no soul stay behind or gets currupted.
  • Poki, Angel of Monsters and Beasts was created to protect and handle magical and non magical monsters and beasts.
  • Loki, Angel of Mischeif was made solely for Onigod entertainment. To create fun and problems in the world.
  • Cupin, Angel of Sin was made to interfere with all creatures to make them not be too good or evil.
The Angels of Flujack, The god of Air, sky and light in order of creation.
  • Sky, Angel of Air was first one of Flujack angels but also the most lazy angel who only needs to make air move unpredictably.
  • Day, the angel of light was created after Lucina, Archangel of Damnation took over Damnation. To take over the responsibility of light.
  • Night, angel of Darkness was create a change of perspective of time and create the calming of darkness.
  • Mr.season, Angel of Seasons was created to change so life has to work more for staying alive.
  • Sandman, Angel of Slumber was made to make creatures sleep. but due to Flujacks love for elves and fey he made them not need sleep.
The Angels of Tellemori, The god of Water, earth and supernatural in order of creation.
  • Plantios, Angel of Flora was made to control and create flora and plants.
  • Lady of Fey, Angel of Fey was created to control the mischievous fey who help with the flora and plants.
  • Lady Love, Angel of Affection was made to make connections and care between creatures as well as reproduction.
  • Arraxiel, Chained angel of consequence forced into angel hood by Drana, Archangel of Dragons and War after trying to slay a dragon on Dragoné, Island of dragons
  • Atheris, Chained angel of Deals and pacts forced into angel-hood as a consequence by Lady of Fey, Angel of Fey because he tricked her.
There are two types of angels; Angels of order and Angels of Parts of life.
Angels of order

The Angels of Order is the Angels who work with the order of The tapestry whilst the Angels of Parts of life are the ones responsible for all the dead and living in The tapestry.
